Session 3

The third session of the expedition follows the party—Mistral Honeygleam, Sir Caldryn Keyne, Scarloc Farmguard, and Tidus Thalorien—as they recover from their first night in the jungle and face the complexities of Chultan law and environmental hazards.

Aftermath and Character Reflections

Following the dinosaur ambush that nearly claimed his life, Sir Caldryn Keyne experiences a rare moment of humility. Usually arrogant and polished, he wakes to find himself covered in mud and fumbling with his gear, struggling to pack his tent as he is completely out of his element. He approaches Mistral Honeygleam and, kneeling in the mud, vows to be better prepared and thanks her for saving his life, acknowledging that his frivolousness previously put the group in danger. Despite this shift, he maintains his "protege" relationship with Scarloc, offering tips on valiant combat even as he fumbles with his own equipment.

Continued Expedition and a Grim Discovery

The party confirms they will continue traveling at a fast pace, hoping to bypass known regions of undead activity. With the guide Eku leading and Scarloc navigating, the group successfully covers two more hexes of dense jungle.

During their journey, they make a somber discovery: the body of an elf hanging from a branch forty feet above the ground. Mistral flies up to investigate and finds a note explaining that the unfortunate explorer had climbed the tree to escape dinosaurs but was too terrified to descend, eventually perishing from his predicament. In a show of respect, the party decides to bury the remains. Caldryn delivers a short eulogy and places two gold coins on the elf's eyes to "pay the ferryman". However, in a moment of levity, Mistral’s flying monkey surreptitiously swaps the gold coins for copper once the body is in the grave.

Their progress was soon challenged when they were ambushed by a pack of three Allosauruses that emerged from the dense undergrowth.

In the ensuing struggle, Scarloc Farmguard landed heavy blows with his greatsword, and Sir Caldryn Keyne utilized his Divine Smite and Warhammer to drive the predators back. Mistral Honeygleam used her magic to shield herself from the dinosaurs' claws before counterattacking with her scimitar, while Tidus Thalorien provided support by chilling the beasts with Ray of Frost. After the party successfully dispatched two of the predators, the final bloodied dinosaur fled into the woods, allowing the group to resume their trek

Encounter with the Flaming Fist

As the party settles in for their third night in the jungle, Tidus uses magic to refill their water containers, ensuring they remain hydrated for the coming day. Their rest is interrupted not by monsters, but by a patrol of a dozen torches. The group is intercepted by the Flaming Fist, a mercenary faction that claims jurisdiction over the region.

The patrol's captain demands to see the party's Exploration Charter, noting that it is illegal to explore or plunder Chult without one. Facing the threat of having their gear confiscated as "contraband," the party engages in tense diplomacy. Caldryn uses his high charisma to influence the soldiers, eventually negotiating a deal:

• The party pays 20 gold upfront toward the 60 gold cost of a charter.

• As collateral for the remaining balance, Caldryn surrenders his ornate Warhammer to the captain.

• Crucially, Caldryn’s weapon is a magical pact weapon, meaning he can simply re-summon it later, effectively "tricking" the patrol into accepting a temporary asset as permanent collateral.

Satisfied for the moment, the Flaming Fist issues the party a charter and allows them to continue their journey toward Mbala.
