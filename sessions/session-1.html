Session 1

The first session of the Tomb of Annihilation module began with the presentation of the worldwide "death curse". This affliction targets everyone who has ever been raised from the dead, causing them to wither and die, and preventing future resurrections.

The Mission Briefing

The player characters—including Mistral Honeygleam (a cheerful, small, level 1 caster), and Sir Caldryn Keyne (an arrogant, well-dressed, wealthy High Elf/Sun Elf paladin)—were gathered at a house in Baldur's Gate. They were acquainted and had worked together previously.

The party's benefactor, Syndra Silvane, revealed she was afflicted by the curse because she had been raised from the dead years ago. She consulted with contacts, including the Harpers, who traced the source of the curse to a necromantic artifact called the Soulmonger. The artifact is believed to be hidden within the ruins of an older civilization deep within Chult.

Syndra Silvane charged the adventurers with traveling to the peninsula of Chult to locate and neutralize the Soulmonger before the curse fully drained her life force. As a reward, she promised each character one rare and one uncommon magic item upon successful completion of the quest.

Departure and Arrival

To expedite travel, Syndra Silvane intended to use a teleport spell to transport the party to the only major settlement in the region, Port Nyanzaru. The characters agreed, though Tidus Thalorien initially preferred to travel by sea.

Upon arrival, the party found themselves in the bustling, colorful, and noisy tropical city of Port Nyanzaru. Syndra Silvane departed to stay with her friend, Merchant Prince Wakanga O'tamu. She recommended the adventurers secure lodging at either the Thundering Lizard (for a "raucous time") or Kaya's House of Repose (for a "good night's sleep"), both located near the Red Bazaar.

Sir Caldryn Keyne resolved to seek lodging at the Thundering Lizard for revelry. Mistral Honeygleam spoke to the harbormaster (described as a large half gold dragon, half man) for directions to the Red Bazaar.

Initial Inquiries and Guide Selection

While in the city, the party began investigating locations and seeking guidance:

  • Wakanga O'tamu: Sir Caldryn Keyne determined that the Merchant Prince Wakanga O'tamu dealt primarily in magic items and kept knowledge/information in scrolls.
  • Guide and Side Quest: While at the inn, a middle-aged woman approached the party, offering her services as a guide. She asked if they were heading toward Mbala. She revealed that she was seeking to eradicate the evil dwelling in the ghost town of Mbala, specifically the hag known as Nanny Pu'pu. The guide offered her services for free if the party agreed to help her destroy this evil. The party accepted this arrangement. The guide agreed to meet them at sunrise the following morning, confirming that she would handle guiding and navigation but the party was responsible for their food and water.

The party purchased essential gear, including insect repellent balm and incense and rain catchers, and planned to start their expedition the next day.
