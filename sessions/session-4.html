Session 4

The fourth session of the expedition saw the party—Mistral Honeygleam, Sir Caldryn Keyne, Scarloc Farmguard, and Tidus Thalorien—grappling with the legalities of their journey, the harsh Chultan climate, and a series of dangerous encounters while navigating toward their objectives.

Legal Disputes and Guided Expertise

Following the previous encounter with the Flaming Fist, Sir Caldryn Keyne questioned their guide, Eku, regarding the legitimacy of their expedition and why she did not already possess a charter. Eku clarified that the Flaming Fist are "illegitimate thugs" not sanctioned by the Merchant Princes of Port Nyanzaru. She explained that obtaining a charter would have required a dangerous trip to Fort Beluarian, which most guides avoid. Despite the tension, the party resolved to continue under her guidance.

Navigation Challenges and a New Destination

The journey through the jungle was hampered by erratic weather and difficult terrain:

  • Environmental Hazards: The group faced alternating days of torrential rainstorms and stifling humidity. Scarloc Farmguard utilized his Tactical Mind to maintain his bearings during the heavy storms.
  • Getting Lost: The party attempted a fast pace but eventually became lost in the dense canopy. After a day of travel, Scarloc realized they had circled back near a previous location.
  • Camp Righteous: Realizing their proximity to the riverbank, the party discussed visiting Camp Righteous. Eku noted the camp is rumored to be abandoned and sits on the edge of undead territory. The party decided to investigate the camp to potentially secure supplies.

Jungle Encounters

The party's journey was interrupted by various denizens of the rainforest:

  • Statue of Ubtao: The group discovered a ten-foot-tall statue of a Chultan God overgrown with vines. Eku identified it as a representation of Ubtao, the "Great Father".
  • Magical Traps and Tracks: Sir Caldryn Keyne used magic to detect a glyph of warding placed on the statue as a trap. Nearby, Scarloc identified extremely large tracks belonging to a massive creature, which the party chose to avoid.
  • The Tiger Ambush: While traveling toward the river, the party was ambushed by a tiger. The group successfully dispatched the predator, with Tidus Thalorien delivering the final blow using a True Strike.
  • Girallons and Stegosaurus: The party spotted a group of four-armed apes (Girallons) sleeping in the trees and successfully used stealth to bypass them. Later, a massive stegosaurus wandered through their camp; finding it to be a peaceful herbivore, the party observed it without conflict.
  • Myconid Sighting: During one night, a small group of myconids riding "mushroom dogs" entered the camp but fled immediately upon seeing the party was well-defended.

The Riverbank Battle

As the party neared the riverbank in a heavy downpour, they were ambushed by six giant spiders.

  • Combat Highlights: The spiders utilized webs to restrain the party, and Scarloc Farmguard was briefly knocked unconscious after being overwhelmed.
  • Arcane and Divine Support: Sir Caldryn Keyne provided critical healing through Lay on Hands and bolstered defenses with Shield of Faith and Armor of Agathys. Mistral Honeygleam and her familiar distracted the creatures, allowing her to land decisive blows with her magic-imbued club.
  • Victory: Through coordinated efforts and Tidus Thalorien’s thalassic magic, the party eventually killed all six spiders, clearing the way to continue toward Camp Righteous.
